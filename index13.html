unfold

Capturing the profound journey of aging, my artwork unfolds like a visual memoir. I employ acrylic paint, on acrylic board and spray paint to craft a narrative that seamlessly transitions from youth to the complexities of later years. Each stroke, guided by technical precision, echoes the emotional depth of my connection to the subject—my grandfather. The intention of the background from white to blue to silver signifies a homage to his childhood all the way to a metaphor that reveals the process of aging. This piece is more than a depiction; it's a contemplative exploration of life's cyclical nature, demonstrating both conceptual depth and technical finesse.

The experimental process for this series involved a deliberate play with transparency and layering. I chose tracing paper for its see-through quality, enabling a dynamic exploration of how different facial expressions accumulate over time. The initial step was painting the entire face on the first layer. Then, with subsequent layers, I strategically added significant color blocks to represent the evolving features—shadows, wrinkles, and nuances that manifest with the passage of time.
