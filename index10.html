Hidden Scent

Using acrylic on canvas, this artwork portrays a broken perfume bottle within a box, its fragrant essence leaking out. The bottle in the box symbolizes hidden memories, isolated yet present as the background. As the scent escapes through the leaking of the liquid, it evokes buried emotions, revealing the enduring power of fragrance to retrack forgotten moments. Through fractured glass and flowing liquid, this piece invites viewers to witness the transformative journey of scent, reevoking hidden chambers of the mind and bringing forth a symphony of nostalgia and introspection.

Another significant challenge I encountered while transferring my sketches to a canvas was the stubbornness of pencil lines on the textured surface. Unlike smooth paper, the coarse canvas made it nearly impossible to erase pencil marks cleanly, leading to persistent smudges and ghost lines that marred the surface. This difficulty forced me to confront and eventually transform my perfectionist tendencies. I realized that I couldn't continually erase and redraw to achieve flawless lines; instead, I had to adapt and accept these imperfections as part of the artwork. This shift in mindset was challenging, as it meant letting go of my desire for absolute precision and embracing a more fluid and forgiving approach to my creative process. I learned to integrate these "mistakes" into the final piece, viewing them not as flaws but as unique characteristics that added to the artwork's authenticity. This experience profoundly changed me as an artist, teaching me the value of flexibility, resilience, and the beauty of imperfection.
